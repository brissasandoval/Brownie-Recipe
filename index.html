Easy Brownie Ingredients

These ingredients are likely to be in your pantry already! It's that easy, and only takes 30 minutes to prepare!

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Cook Time: 20 Minutes

Servings: 16

Just 8 ingredients needed:

  • Sugar: These easy brownies start with two cups of white sugar.
  • Flour: 1 1/2 Cups of All-purpose
  • Butter: Two sticks of melted butter give the brownies moisture and richness.
  • Eggs: 4 eggs lend even more moisture. Plus, they help bind the batter together.
  • Cocoa powder: Of course, you'll need 1/2 cup of cocoa powder for chocolate brownies!
  • Vanilla: 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract enhances the overall flavor of the brownies.
  • Baking Powder: 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder acts as a leavener, which means it helps the brownies rise.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt (1/2 teaspoon) enhances the flavors of the other ingredients.

The Steps to Make Easy Bronwies

You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below but here's a brief overview of what you can expect:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined evenly.
  3. Spread the batter into a non stick prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes; cool for 10 minutes before slicing into squares.

How to Store Brownies

Store the brownies in an airtight container for 3 to 4 days at room temperature or for up to one week in the refrigerator. You can freeze brownies for up to three months.
